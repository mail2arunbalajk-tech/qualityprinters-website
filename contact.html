Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ depends on the product — printed cartons and mono boxes typically start from 1,000 pieces, and paper bags from 500 pieces. We also take up smaller batches for select products. WhatsApp us your requirement and we'll confirm the exact MOQ for your item.

How long does production and delivery take?

Standard orders are typically ready in 7–10 working days after artwork approval. Larger or speciality orders may take longer — we always confirm the timeline with your quote. Urgent requirements can often be accommodated, so do ask.

Do you help with design and artwork?

Yes. If you have print-ready files, we work with those directly. If not, our team helps you with layout, sizing and design adjustments so your packaging comes out exactly as intended. Just share your logo and content to get started.

Do you deliver outside Coimbatore?

Yes — we deliver pan India. We are based in New Siddhapudur, Coimbatore, and regularly ship to customers across Tamil Nadu and the rest of India through reliable logistics partners.

What are your payment terms?

We normally take an advance to begin production, with the balance payable on dispatch or delivery. Exact terms are confirmed along with your quotation. We are a GST-registered business (GSTIN: 33BJXPD2473D1Z9) and provide proper tax invoices.

Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

Yes, samples can be arranged for most products before bulk production so you can check the material, print quality and finish. Contact us on WhatsApp at +91 96981 54825 to request a sample.

Do you make pharmaceutical packaging with regulatory requirements?

Yes — pharmaceutical mono cartons and strip packs are one of our specialities. We print batch details, regulatory text and barcodes to your specification, with the accuracy and consistency pharma packaging demands.
