Our Story

About Quality Prints

A family-run packaging business built on craftsmanship, precision, and a deep commitment to our clients' brands.

Founded in Coimbatore

Packaging excellence, rooted in Tamil Nadu

Quality Prints and Paper Bags was established in Coimbatore with a single mission: to provide businesses across India with packaging that is as good as the products inside. What started as a modest printing operation has grown into a full-service custom packaging facility trusted by over 500 brands.

We operate from our facility at New Siddhapudur, Coimbatore — in the heart of Tamil Nadu's manufacturing belt — with access to premium materials, fast logistics, and a skilled team dedicated to precision at every step.

From pharmaceutical mono cartons requiring regulatory precision to premium gift boxes crafted for luxury brands, we cover the entire spectrum of packaging needs under one roof.
