Custom Packaging Manufacturer in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu

Quality Prints and Paper Bags is a leading custom packaging manufacturer based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, serving businesses across India. With over 15 years of experience in the printing and packaging industry, we specialise in custom printed boxes, mono cartons, pharmaceutical packaging, food-grade boxes, kraft paper bags, and specialty packaging for a wide range of industries.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in New Siddhapudur, Coimbatore is equipped with advanced offset printing presses, die-cutting machines, lamination units, and automated box gluing lines — enabling us to handle both small-batch orders and large enterprise volumes with consistent quality and fast turnaround times.

Why Businesses Choose Quality Prints for Bulk Packaging Orders

We are the preferred packaging partner for enterprises, FMCG brands, pharmaceutical companies, food manufacturers, herbal brands, and retail chains across South India. Our competitive advantages include:

  • In-house end-to-end manufacturing — no outsourcing, full quality control
  • Custom dieline creation and artwork support for new product launches
  • Multiple printing finishes: gloss lamination, matte lamination, UV coating, foil stamping
  • GST-registered enterprise B2B invoicing (GST: 33BJXPD2473D1Z9)
  • Pan-India supply with reliable dispatch from Coimbatore
  • Dedicated account management for bulk and repeat clients

Pharmaceutical Packaging Supplier in Coimbatore

We manufacture high-quality pharmaceutical cartons, strip cartons, medicine box packaging, and doctor sample packaging for pharma brands and distributors. Our pharma packaging solutions comply with standard industry requirements for text legibility, material quality, and dimensional accuracy.

Food-Grade Custom Box Printing in Tamil Nadu

Our food packaging solutions include custom printed boxes for flour, butter, spices, bakery products, restaurant take-away packaging, chicken/snack boxes, and more. All food contact materials are sourced from approved suppliers to meet FSSAI packaging guidelines.
